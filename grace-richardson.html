Meet Grace Richardson

Grace is Pickle Gallery’s wellness advocate, ensuring every expedition balances creative drive with physical and mental health. A certified yoga instructor and nutritionist, she designs trail‑friendly meal plans and dawn‑patrol stretching routines that keep the team energized. Grace’s “mindful movement” sessions encourage photographers to slow down, breathe, and notice subtle details otherwise overlooked. Her compassionate leadership fosters a crew culture where vulnerability is celebrated and burnout is out of the picture. Grace reminds everyone: thriving artists are healthy artists.

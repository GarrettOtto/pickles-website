Meet Ridge Poll

Ridge is Pickle Gallery’s trailblazing cartographer, renowned for transforming GPS data into interactive maps that guide followers through the team’s adventures. With degrees in geography and data visualization, he overlays topographic contours, wildlife migration routes, and historical landmarks to create multilayered digital experiences. Ridge’s maps are more than navigational aids—they are narrative canvases that invite users to zoom, click, and uncover hidden stories. His field days often involve drone‑based photogrammetry, capturing terrain from above to complement ground‑level artistry. Ridge’s motto: the landscape is a living library—every ridge, river, and rock etches a chapter waiting to be read.

Meet Ridge Poll

Taken

Ridge Poll is the athletic powerhouse of the Pickles friend group, bringing energy, team spirit, and a competitive edge to all our activities. His dedication to sports and fitness inspires everyone around him, while his genuine team-player mentality ensures that no one feels left out. Ridge's positive attitude and ability to motivate others make him an essential part of our adventures, whether we're hiking challenging trails or engaging in friendly competitions.

About Me

I've been passionate about sports and physical activities since I was a kid, playing everything from basketball and football to track and field. My competitive nature is balanced by my belief that teamwork and sportsmanship are what truly matter. When I'm not training or competing, I enjoy coaching youth sports and helping others discover their athletic potential. I believe that physical activity is not just about staying fit, but also about building character, discipline, and meaningful connections with others. Being part of the Pickles group has given me the perfect outlet to combine my love for sports with the joy of friendship.

Fun Facts

  • I've completed three marathons and one ultra-marathon
  • I played varsity basketball all four years of high school
  • I've summited four 14,000+ foot peaks
  • I can do 100 push-ups without stopping

How I Joined Pickles

I became part of the Pickles group during my sophomore year of college. I was looking for a group of friends who shared my enthusiasm for outdoor activities and adventures. I met Noah during an intramural basketball game, and we instantly connected over our competitive spirit and love for sports. He invited me to join a weekend hiking trip with his friends, and that's where I met the rest of the Pickles crew. What impressed me most was how everyone supported each other on that challenging trail - it wasn't about who reached the summit first, but about making sure everyone had an amazing experience. That sense of camaraderie and teamwork resonated deeply with me, and I knew I had found my people. Since then, I've been an active member of the group, often organizing sports competitions and physical challenges that bring us all together. The Pickles group has become like family to me, pushing me to be my best while accepting me exactly as I am.

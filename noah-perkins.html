Meet Noah Perkins

Noah is the perpetual optimist of Pickle Gallery—an explorer whose enthusiasm is as boundless as the landscapes he photographs. An early fascination with cartography led him to chart remote backcountry routes before sunrise, camera at the ready. Noah’s work is distinguished by rich textures and quiet, contemplative compositions that whisper the stories of hidden valleys and forgotten byways. When he is not trekking with a tripod strapped to his pack, he mentors aspiring adventurers, teaching essential orienteering skills and championing environmental stewardship. His mantra, “leave trails better than you found them,” echoes through every project he undertakes.

Meet Garrett Otto

Available

Hello! I'm Garrett, the resident stargazer and outdoor enthusiast of the Pickles crew. I'm passionate about exploring Utah's natural wonders, from its breathtaking mountain trails to its crystal-clear night skies. You'll often find me with my telescope capturing celestial wonders or planning our group's next adventure. I'm usually the one with a backpack full of trail mix, a camera ready for the perfect sunset shot, and somehow, always an extra water bottle for whoever forgot theirs.

About Me

As a third-year student at the University of Utah, I balance my academic life with my passion for outdoor exploration and astronomy. I've developed a keen eye for astrophotography, capturing stunning images of constellations, planets, and distant galaxies. When I'm not gazing at the stars, I'm usually planning our next group hiking expedition or scouting new camping spots in Utah's diverse landscapes. I volunteer at the local planetarium twice a month, helping visitors discover the wonders of our night sky. My friends know me as the go-to person for trail recommendations, stargazing spots, and adventure planning—always ready with a map and a plan!

Fun Facts

  • Amateur astrophotographer with a collection of galaxy photos
  • Hiked over 20 different trails in Utah's national parks
  • Can make pizza from scratch, including the dough
  • Visited 12 states and counting

How I Joined

My Pickles journey began during a stargazing event I organized on campus back in 2022. I had set up my telescope near the quad when Chance and Ridge wandered over, curious about what I was doing. We spent hours that night talking about constellations, sharing stories, and eventually planning a hiking trip for the following weekend. That hike turned into a regular tradition, and soon our small group expanded as everyone brought along friends who just seemed to click with our vibe. The name "Pickles" came from an inside joke during our first group camping trip—I had packed an entire jar of pickles as my "essential survival food" which became the unexpected hero ingredient in our improvised campfire meals after we forgot half our planned groceries. From that moment on, we were the Pickles crew, and I couldn't imagine my college experience without this incredible group of friends who turn even ordinary days into adventures.

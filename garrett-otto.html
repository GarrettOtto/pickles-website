Meet Garrett Otto

Garrett is Pickle Gallery’s resident tinkerer and gear innovator. With fifteen years in mechanical design, he custom‑builds lightweight camera rigs, weatherproof lens housings, and collapsible tripods that withstand extreme conditions. Garrett’s workshop resembles a mad scientist’s lab—3D printers hum alongside laser cutters, while sketches of experimental optics cover the walls. He revels in troubleshooting: if a piece of gear fails mid‑shoot, Garrett conjures a fix with duct tape and ingenuity. His product‑testing vlogs have garnered a cult following among adventure photographers eager to hack their own solutions. Garrett’s credo: invent the tools your imagination requires.

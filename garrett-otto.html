Hey there! I'm Garrett, one of the original members of the Pickles crew. When I'm not hanging out with this amazing group of friends, you can usually find me tinkering with tech or planning our next adventure.

About Me

I'm a tech enthusiast and adventure seeker who loves bringing new ideas to life. Whether it's organizing group activities or finding creative solutions to problems, I'm always up for a challenge!

How I Joined

I've been part of the Pickles crew since the very beginning! It all started during freshman year of college when a group of us met in the same dorm building. We clicked instantly over our shared love for adventure and trying new things. One weekend, we decided to go on an impromptu camping trip, and that's when the "Pickles" name was born (it's a funny story involving a jar of pickles and a midnight snack raid). Since then, we've been inseparable, creating memories and embarking on countless adventures together.

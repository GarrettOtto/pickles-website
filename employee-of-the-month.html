Employee of the Month — Chance Wiese

When excellence meets unwavering dedication, you get Chance Wiese — our Employee of the Month!

This month, Chance didn’t just meet expectations; he re‑engineered them. While juggling his usual responsibilities as our master meteorologist and “Mr. Golden Hour,” Chance single‑handedly architected, coded, and deployed the entire Pickles website overhaul. From crafting pixel‑perfect layouts to integrating dynamic JavaScript features, he turned long wish‑lists into working reality, all while keeping the team laughing and inspired.

Chance’s code commits landed at lightning speed, but the care behind them was anything but rushed. He documented every function, optimized every query, and even squeezed in a sleek dark‑mode toggle at 2 a.m. because, in his words, “users deserve elegance at any hour.” Beyond code, Chance coordinated late‑night stand‑ups across time zones, ensured design consistency, and made sure our server never missed a beat during launch week.

In short, our stunning new digital home — complete with responsive galleries, staff pages, and those easter‑egg hover effects you keep discovering — exists because Chance decided it should. For embodying curiosity, craftsmanship, and camaraderie, we salute him. Congratulations, Chance! The Pickles team (and the internet) thank you.

Our Group Activities

From sports to stargazing, here are some of the amazing activities we love to do together as friends. Join us for unforgettable moments and endless fun!

Weekly Pickleball

Our signature activity! Every Thursday evening, we meet at the local courts for intense matches and friendly competition. All skill levels welcome - from beginners to our resident pro Grace! Join us for some rallies, laughs, and maybe even learn Chance's famous spin serve.

Host: Grace Weekly

Beach Sports

When the weather's warm, we head to the sand for volleyball, spikeball, and frisbee. Our epic tournaments are known for both competitive spirit and endless laughter. Ridge's diving saves are a must-see!

Host: Ridge Seasonal

Weekend Adventures

Monthly outdoor excursions to explore Utah's beautiful landscapes. Whether it's hiking in Moab, camping under the stars, or rock climbing at local spots, these trips create our most memorable moments. Noah's trail mix is legendary!

Host: Chance Monthly

Stargazing Nights

Once a month, we escape the city lights for a night under the stars. Garrett brings his telescope, and we spend hours spotting constellations, sharing stories, and contemplating the universe (with hot chocolate, of course!).

Host: Garrett Monthly

Photo Adventures

Julia leads our photography outings where we capture stunning landscapes and candid moments. From sunset shoots to urban exploration, we're always finding new perspectives and creating lasting memories.

Host: Ellie Bi-weekly

Baking Sessions

Abbie hosts amazing baking sessions where we try new recipes and perfect old favorites. From cookies to complex cakes, it's always a delicious adventure. The best part? Taste testing everything at the end!

Host: Abbie Monthly

Creative Workshops

Ashlyn leads various creative sessions - from painting and crafting to DIY projects. It's a chance to explore our artistic sides and make something unique. The results are always interesting, to say the least!

Host: Ashlyn Monthly

Game Nights

Spencer hosts our legendary game nights every other Saturday. From strategic board games like Catan and Ticket to Ride to hilarious party games like Codenames and Jackbox, there's something for everyone. McKenly always brings the best snacks!

Host: Spencer Bi-weekly

Holiday Traditions

From our annual Halloween costume contest to our epic Christmas gift exchange, we love celebrating together. Each holiday brings its own special traditions that make our friendship even stronger.

Host: Everyone Monthly

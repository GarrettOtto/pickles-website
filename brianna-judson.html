Meet Brianna Judson

Available

Hello! I'm Brianna, the creative soul and culinary enthusiast of the Pickles crew. With a passion for art, cooking, and community building, I bring a splash of color and flavor to our adventures. Whether I'm organizing a themed potluck dinner, sketching landscapes during our hikes, or leading a community art project, I believe in making every moment a masterpiece. My philosophy is that creativity connects us all, and I'm always looking for new ways to inspire our friend group's collective imagination.

About Me

  • Hometown: Portland, Oregon
  • Occupation: Graphic Designer & Food Blogger
  • Joined Pickles: March 2023
  • Favorite Activity: Hosting creative workshops and cooking classes

Fun Facts

  • I've visited 17 countries and collected a recipe from each one
  • I can play the ukulele and often bring it to our beach gatherings
  • I once won a regional chili cook-off with my secret family recipe
  • I maintain a rooftop garden with over 30 varieties of herbs and vegetables

How I Joined Pickles

I met the Pickles crew at a community art festival where I was running a collaborative mural project. We bonded over shared creative interests and their enthusiasm for my homemade pickle-inspired appetizers (yes, really!). After joining them for a few weekend adventures, I was officially invited to become part of the Pickles family during a memorable sunset picnic where everyone contributed something handmade. I brought my signature watercolor cookies, and the rest is history!

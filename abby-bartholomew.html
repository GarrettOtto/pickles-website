Meet Abby Bartholomew

Abby wields drone technology with painterly finesse, capturing sweeping bird’s‑eye visuals that reveal patterns invisible from the ground. A certified UAV pilot and aerospace engineer, she designs custom flight paths to minimize wildlife disturbance and maximize aesthetic impact. Abby’s rotating aerial series, “Textures of Earth,” invites audiences to contemplate the world’s geometric beauty—from braided river deltas to mosaic salt flats. Her workshops demystify drone regulations and ethics, empowering responsible aerial artistry. Abby’s creed: flight should deepen our appreciation for the planet, not distance us from it.

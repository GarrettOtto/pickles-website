Meet Julia Wasden

Available

Meet Julia, our resident photographer and creative visionary who captures the essence of every Pickles adventure through her lens. With an eye for detail and a passion for storytelling through images, Julia transforms our ordinary moments into extraordinary memories that we'll cherish for years to come.

About Me

I've always believed that a photograph is more than just an image—it's a moment frozen in time, a story waiting to be told. As the photographer of our group, I love documenting our adventures and the genuine connections we share. When I'm not behind the camera, I'm exploring new hiking trails, experimenting with different artistic mediums, or planning our next photo adventure. I find beauty in the smallest details and believe that life's most precious moments often happen in the spaces between planned events—those spontaneous laughs, quiet conversations, and unexpected discoveries that make our friendship so special.

Fun Facts

  • Won three local photography competitions with candid shots of our Pickles adventures
  • Traveled to 12 countries documenting different cultures through street photography
  • Can identify the origin of coffee beans just by their aroma—a skill developed during my barista days
  • Specializes in astrophotography and leads our stargazing nights with Garrett

How I Joined Pickles

My journey with the Pickles group began during a local photography exhibition where I had displayed some of my landscape work. Grace and Garrett stopped by my booth, and we instantly connected over our shared love for capturing Utah's natural beauty. They mentioned they were heading to a sunset hike with friends later that week and invited me to join them—both as a participant and to document the experience. I remember being nervous about meeting a whole group of new people, but that anxiety melted away within minutes of arriving. Everyone was so welcoming, genuinely interested in my photography, and excited to have their adventures documented. That evening, as we watched the sun paint the sky in brilliant hues of orange and pink, I knew I had found not just subjects for my camera, but true friends who appreciated both the art and the artist. Since then, our bi-weekly photo adventures have become a cornerstone of the Pickles experience, and I've watched our collection of memories grow with each click of my shutter.

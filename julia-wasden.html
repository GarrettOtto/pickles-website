Meet Julia Wasden

Julia is the multilingual storyteller who lends Pickle Gallery its global voice. Fluent in five languages, she deftly translates interviews and signage, allowing the team to converse respectfully across cultures. Julia’s essays combine anthropological insight with poetic flair, weaving local folklore and socio‑historical context into each photo essay. Her passion for cultural preservation drives partnerships with grassroots organizations, ensuring featured communities benefit directly from increased visibility. Julia’s guiding principle: every culture is a universe—approach with humility, curiosity, and gratitude.

Meet McKenly Day

Taken

McKenly Day is our adventure coordinator extraordinaire, with a natural talent for bringing people together and creating unforgettable experiences. Her enthusiasm for exploration and discovery is contagious, making every Pickles outing an exciting journey. Whether planning outdoor adventures or organizing social gatherings, McKenly's attention to detail and genuine care for others ensures that everyone feels included and valued.

About Me

I'm an explorer at heart with a passion for bringing people together through shared experiences. My friends would describe me as energetic, thoughtful, and always ready for the next adventure. When I'm not coordinating activities for our Pickles group, you can find me hiking new trails, experimenting with photography, or planning my next road trip. I believe that the best memories are created when we step outside our comfort zones and try new things together. My background in event planning helps me organize our group activities, but it's the genuine connections and friendships that make each experience truly special.

Fun Facts

  • I've visited 18 national parks and counting
  • I can set up a tent in under 3 minutes
  • My longest hike was 24 miles in a single day
  • I keep a color-coded planner for all our group activities

How I Joined Pickles

I joined the Pickles group during my sophomore year of college when I was looking to expand my social circle and find people who shared my love for adventure. I met Noah at a campus outdoor club meeting, and he mentioned this amazing group of friends who were planning a weekend camping trip. Always up for new experiences, I decided to join them. From the moment we all gathered around the campfire that first night, I knew I had found my people. Everyone was so welcoming and genuine, sharing stories and laughing together like old friends. What started as a one-time outing quickly turned into regular adventures, and before I knew it, I was helping to plan our activities and bringing new ideas to the group. The Pickles crew has become like family to me, and I can't imagine my college experience without these incredible friends and the memories we've created together.

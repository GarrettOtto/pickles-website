Meet Konner Clawson

Available

Meet Konner, the strategic mind behind our game nights and a founding member of the Pickles friend group. With his quick wit, analytical thinking, and genuine enthusiasm for bringing people together, Konner adds a unique dynamic to our adventures and creates memorable experiences for everyone involved.

About Me

I'm a strategic thinker who loves the challenge of a good game, whether it's chess, video games, or planning our next group adventure. When I'm not organizing game nights for the Pickles crew, you can find me exploring new technologies, perfecting my gaming skills, or enjoying the outdoors with friends. I believe that the best moments in life come from meaningful connections and shared experiences, which is why I'm passionate about bringing people together through fun, engaging activities.

Fun Facts

  • Undefeated chess champion in our friend group for three years running
  • Collector of vintage video games with over 200 titles
  • Completed the challenging Angels Landing hike at Zion National Park
  • Makes the best homemade pizza in the group according to our annual taste test

How I Joined Pickles

My journey with the Pickles group began during a local gaming tournament where I met Noah and Chance. What started as friendly competition quickly evolved into genuine friendship as we discovered our shared interests beyond gaming. They invited me to a weekend camping trip with their friend group, and I immediately felt at home with this welcoming bunch of diverse personalities. I remember bringing my portable chess set to that first outing, which sparked an impromptu tournament that lasted well into the night under starlight. That weekend solidified my place in the group, and I've been organizing our game nights and strategic adventures ever since. Being part of Pickles has expanded my horizons beyond games and introduced me to lifelong friends who accept me for exactly who I am.

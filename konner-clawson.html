Meet Konner Clawson

Konner is the logistical wizard who transforms Pickle Gallery’s wild ideas into carefully choreographed expeditions. With a background in supply‑chain management and wilderness medicine, he crafts itineraries that balance bold exploration with pragmatic risk mitigation. Konner’s spreadsheets map everything from pack weight distribution to satellite‑phone check‑ins, yet his steady demeanor keeps the mood light even when plans shift. Around campfires, he regales the crew with tales of historical explorers and the lessons they offer modern creatives. Thanks to Konner, every journey runs like clockwork—freeing the team to chase spontaneity without sacrificing safety.
